Overview

The dataset contained noisy slides with stains and artifacts. We prioritized automated cleaning, ROI-centric tile extraction, and a slide-level MIL architecture that could aggregate weak patch signals into a holistic prediction.

Preprocessing highlights

  • HSV-based quality control to remove slime and artifact-heavy slides.
  • ROI mask usage to center tiles on diagnostically relevant regions.
  • Multi-scale tiling (context 768px, detail 256px) with paired masks.

Modeling highlights

  • Attention-MIL with ViT-S backbone via timm.
  • Slide-level aggregation to reduce patch ambiguity.
  • Two-phase fine-tuning with low LR and CosineAnnealingLR.

Pipeline

Progression from baseline CNNs to slide-level MIL.

1. QC + ROI extraction

HSV filtering and ROI masks to keep diagnostically relevant tissue.

2. Multi-scale tiling

Context and detail tiles with masks saved to structured train/test CSVs.

3. Model evolution

Baseline ResNet-18, ROI+multi-scale ResNet, then Attention-MIL.

4. Inference

Slide-level aggregation and submission generation.
