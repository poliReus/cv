Overview

The platform surfaces penalty outcomes through structured team pages. Each player page overlays shot locations on a goal image and links to video evidence. A calendar module tracks fixture progression.

Core features

  • Login-protected access to analytics views.
  • Team and player navigation with branded assets.
  • Shot map overlay with video links per penalty.
  • Calendar module for match schedule awareness.

Data representation

  • Penalty spots plotted as absolute goal coordinates.
  • Outcome encoded via goal/miss icons.
  • Result tables with match, minute, and clip links.
